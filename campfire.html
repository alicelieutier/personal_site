About Campfire Circle

For millennia humans have gathered around fires to tell stories and connect. In this event the fire is symbolic but the connections are real.

Campfire circle is not a yoga practice or a networking event. It is a unique experience where we dive into our emotions and create bond based on vulnerability and trust.

Genuine connections are formed when we see the human in eachother. Through breathwork, introspection and active listening, we'll practice holding space for each others experiences and emotions.

Whether you want to improve your emotional intelligence skills for work, or want to experience real human connections, come with an open mind and heart, and leave with a renewed sense of self and community.
